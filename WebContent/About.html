About Us

At Chicago Tourism, we give you the best service available so that you can have a great time with your loved ones. Chicago Tourism started 20 years ago, in 1996, with one primary objective - To make the beautiful place we call home, available for everyone. Since then, we have gone on to be the primary source of information for tourists from around the world.

Our goal is to make sure you and your loved ones feel at home even while being away from home. To make this possible, we provide the list of all the places to visit in this beautiful city. With a wide list of places and reviews from people of different ethnicities and places, Chicago Tourism provieds a comprehensive experience for you, the Customer.

We believe that Chicago Tourism is the best in the business because of these factors :

  • Experience – 25 years experience means we have learnt a thing or two.
  • Customer Satisfaction.
  • Comprehensive Details and Information.
  • Continual improvements to the Information provided.
